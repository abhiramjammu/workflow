05 Image Workflow

The Layered Asset Approach

Raw generation is only the foundation. True quality comes from granular control.

  • AI Generation — Gemini, Whisk & GrokGenerating the base image using Gemini with structured prompts, then rapidly remixing variations in Whisk until the exact creative direction is achieved.
  • Layering for Full ControlSeparating generated outputs into subject, foreground, and background layers using Mixboard, enabling independent control without regenerating.
  • Post-Production Built for ConversionFinal compositing in Photoshop corrects lighting and adds depth-of-field. The goal is crafting assets real enough to stop a scroll and drive conversions.

06 Video Generation

Transforming high-fidelity images into dynamic visual narratives and live interactive prototypes.

AI Motion from Layered Stills

Feeding separated layers into Kling AI or Gemini video with precise motion prompts. The AI animates each layer independently for a natural parallax effect.

Controlling What the AI Does

Testing variations of motion prompts to specify speed and camera drift. Subtle, organic movement is prioritized over robotic or floaty generation.

Cut for the Platform, Built to Convert

Grading and formatting the final clip in Premiere Pro and After Effects. Every edit is specifically tailored for Reels, ads, or stories to maximize engagement.

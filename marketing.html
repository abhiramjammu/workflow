Pipeline & Methodology

01

SEO & Intent Mapping

Instead of blind content creation, we reverse-engineer high-intent search queries using Ahrefs and Search Console. We build structured topic clusters that naturally capture user search behavior, ensuring organic traffic is highly qualified before they even see an ad.

Result: Higher Domain Authority & Qualified Leads
02

A/B Testing Content Vectors

We deploy AI-generated creatives in rapid micro-campaigns on Meta and Google Ads. By running strict multivariate tests (testing hook against hook, visual against visual), we rely purely on CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) data to decide which creative gets the massive scaling budget.

Result: Lower CPA & Eliminated Guesswork
03

Frictionless Funnel Architecture

Using GA4 heatmaps and drop-off analysis, we identify exactly where users lose interest. We continuously iterate the UI/UX of the landing pages to remove friction, ensuring that the traffic we pay for actually converts into users.

Result: Maximized Conversion Rates
